IJMTR's Representative Network Programme

If you�re interested in honoring your knowledge and skills by supporting & promoting research activity in your University/College with the support of one of the world's leading International Research Journal, you�ll be pleased to know that International Journal of Research in Informative Science Application & Techniques (IJMTR)has launched the IJMTR​ RNP (Representative Network Programme) in September-2017

This Programme will serves as the foundation for the new research & innovations in the fields of new trends and developments around the world.

International Journal of Multidisciplinary Technological Research (IJMTR) welcomes expressions of interest in joining our Representative Network, we are presently seeking representatives around the world.

Who can Join:

Professors/ Lecturers/ Existing IJMTR​ Reviewers/ Authors who would like to be considered in becoming an representative of IJMTR can apply on this link http://www.ijmtr.com/ems_apply_reviewer.html .

  • Listing in, and access to, the online IJMTR Representative Network Directory
  • Demonstrate your professional status with Official IJMTR Representative Certification (At your University/College)
  • Get official IJMTR Representative ID Proof (Welcome Kit)
  • Significant discounts on IJMTR's peer-reviewed journals.
  • Access to IJMTR Research Resources
  • Special Discounts on IJMTR Publications and other Services
  • Gain professional recognition as official IJMTR Representative and demonstrate your excellence in the practice of research related activities.

  • Get entry & access to IJMTR Hosted Conferences, Events, Seminars & Webinars
  • IJMTR Awards Program, acknowledging outstanding professional contributions made by you and your colleagues.
  • Meet and network with a diverse range of members in your area & around the world
  • First-to-know status for IJMTR's programs and calls for papers.
  • Connect with other international members through a range of networking activities
  • Professional Development and Recognition
  • Financial aid and scholarship opportunities
  • Get in touch with cut-in-edge technology, research activities & trends around the globe.

As an IJMTR Representative, you can take advantage of extensive professional and student benefits, most of which are available at no additional cost or at a substantial discount.

Joining IJMTRis the most important connection a researcher can make. Members enjoy a host of valuable benefits, plus the opportunity to have a direct impact on the engineering research work.

The information submitted using below will be used by IJMTR Editorial board to determine your suitability for appointment as an official representative of IJMTR (India) at your affiliated university/college.

You will be notified of our decision by email after your Academic Profile Form and any supporting documents have been assessed by our editorial board and only if your application has been successful.
